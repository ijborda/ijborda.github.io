Hello! I am Imari Joy C. Borda.
I am passionate about mathematics, reasearch, and coding.

I am a Science Research Specialist at the Center for Applied Modeling, Data Analytics, and Bioinformatics for Decision Support Systems in Health (AMDABiDSS-Health) of the University of the Philippines Mindanao. I am under the project Predictive Modeling and Viral Phylodynamic Analysis on the Spatial and Temporal Patterns of Disease Outbreaks with considerations for Control and Logistics (PPASTOL).

My research entails the study and the implementation of phylodynamics in the context of SARS-CoV-2 and Dengue using publicly available genomic sequences.

I code in Matlab, R and know some Python (it's been a while since I used this). On the side, I am learning more programming languages such as HTML and Javascript.

I also sometimes write blogs/guides about certain mathematical topics.
